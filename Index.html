Good to Know

Is there a dress code for the events?

While your presence is what matters most to us and dress codes are entirely optional, we would love for you to join in the colorful spirit of our celebrations if you wish!

  • Haldi: Let's paint the town yellow! We suggest a yellow Kurta for the men, and a yellow Kurti or Saree for the women.
  • Sangeet: Get ready to dance comfortably. We recommend a Kurta with a Chunni/Stole for men, and a Lehenga or Party Saree for women.
  • The Wedding: Please feel free to wear any traditional Indian attire that you love as we take our vows.

Note on Weather: Please note that summer temperatures in Wardha get quite high in May. We highly suggest wearing light, breathable, and cooler fabrics so you can stay comfortable and enjoy the celebrations to the fullest!

What should we expect at the Sangeet?

The Sangeet is all about celebrating through music, laughter, and dance! Expect a magical evening filled with choreographed family performances, a lively open dance floor, and delicious food.

Don't worry if you aren't a professional dancer—just bring your enthusiasm, comfortable dancing shoes, and get ready to make some unforgettable memories with us!

Will the events be indoors or outdoors?

The Mehndi and Haldi ceremonies will be held separately at the bride's and groom's respective houses under a beautiful outdoor mandap (the Mehndi will be a lovely evening affair).

The Sangeet will take place outside on the open lawn at our venue, while the Wedding ceremony will be hosted indoors in a fully air-conditioned hall.

As a reminder, Wardha gets quite warm in May, so we recommend preparing accordingly and wearing light clothing for the outdoor events!

Will rooms be provided at the venue?

Yes, we have arranged rooms at the venue for our family and friends! However, because the venue has a limited number of rooms, we kindly request our friends to adjust and share rooms with one another.

Other hotels are quite far from the venue, and we really don't want you to miss out on any of the late-night wedding festivities! We will try our absolute best to accommodate everyone comfortably, but we sincerely apologize in advance if things get a little cozy. We truly appreciate your understanding and cooperation. Thank you!
